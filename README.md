# Phishing Report – Reward Scam Email

**Author:** Biruk Mesfin  
**Date:** March 26, 2026  

---

## Overview

This repository contains the analysis of a phishing email categorized as a **Reward Scam**. The report examines the email’s content, sender details, attachments, and technical headers to determine its legitimacy and risk level. The purpose of this analysis is to demonstrate phishing identification techniques and provide actionable insights for security awareness.

---

## Contents

The PDF report  includes:

1. **Executive Summary** – Overview of the reward scam phishing email, potential threats, and malicious tactics.  
2. **Introduction** – Explanation of phishing attacks and their impact on individuals and organizations.  
3. **Scope of the Report** – Description of the analysis approach and limitations.  
4. **Phishing Background Overview** – Types of phishing attacks, risks, and examples.  
5. **Tools and Methodology** – Technical tools used (Google Header Analyzer, MXToolbox) and the analysis workflow.  
6. **Phishing Email Sample Analysis** – Detailed examination of the reward scam email including:  
   - Phishing indicators  
   - Sender and domain analysis  
   - Attachment and link inspection  
   - Email authentication results (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)  
7. **Key Indicators of Phishing** – Observed patterns and techniques used by attackers.  
8. **Risk Classification** – Risk level assigned to the email sample.  
9. **Conclusion** – Final findings and security recommendations.
